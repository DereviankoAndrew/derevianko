Global Risks

Peace and disarmament problem

The problem of peace and disarmament - the problem of preventing a third world war remains the most important, the most priority problem of mankind. In the second half of the XX century, nuclear weapons appeared and there was a real threat of destruction of entire countries and even continents, i.e. practically all modern life.

Ecological problem

Ecological problem - degradation of the global ecological system, as a result of irrational use of natural resources and its pollution with wastes of human activity.

Fuel and raw materials problem

Fuel and raw materials problem - the problem of reliable provision of mankind with fuel and energy, as a result of the rapid growth in the consumption of natural mineral resources.

Food problem

Food problem - according to FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization) and WHO (World Health Organization), between 0.8 and 1.2 billion people are hungry and malnourished in the world.

Using the resources of the oceans

Using the resources of the oceans - at all stages of human civilization, the World Ocean was one of the most important sources of maintaining life on Earth. At present, the ocean is not just a single natural space, but also a natural and economic system.

Peaceful space exploration

Peaceful space exploration - Space is a global environment, a common heritage of mankind. Testing of various weapons can threaten the entire planet at once. "Littering" and "littering" of outer space.

Overcoming the underdevelopment of developing countries

Overcoming the underdevelopment of developing countries - most of the world's population lives in poverty and misery, which can be considered extreme forms of backwardness. Per capita income in some countries is less than $ 1 a day.
